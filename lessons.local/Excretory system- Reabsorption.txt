EXCRETORY SYSTEM: REABSORPTION
Author(s): V idya Pillai, SHRADDHA BHURKUNDE, Sugat Dabholkar
Subject: Biology, Self-directed
Time: 4 class periods (35 minutes each)
Level: High School / Freshmen Biology
Version: v2
OVERVIEW
This lesson is about reabsorption in nephron. Students will use computational model to study absorption, selective
reabsorption and impact of changing lumen size on absorption in kidney.
